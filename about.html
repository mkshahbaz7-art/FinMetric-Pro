About FinMetric Pro

Simple, beautiful, and customizable online calculators.

Our Mission

We created FinMetric Pro because we got tired of bloated, ad-filled calculator websites that are slow and hard to use on mobile. Our goal is to provide clean, fast, and beautiful tools that just work — no distractions, no tracking, no nonsense.

Open & Free

All of our calculators are completely free to use. The FinMetric Pro calculators were technically conceived, coded, and build by Mohammad Shahbaz Khan, a coding and finance enthusiast. All our codes are open-source, and we publish them on GitHub.
