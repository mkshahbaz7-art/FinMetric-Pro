Privacy Policy

FinMetric Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All calculations happen in your browser only. Nothing is sent to any server (except optional Google Analytics page views, which are completely anonymous and contain no personal information).

We do not use cookies for tracking, do not ask for your name, email, or phone number, and do not sell any data because we never collect it in the first place.

If you contact us via the Contact page, your message is received only by the site owner and is never shared with third parties.
